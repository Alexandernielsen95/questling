Questling — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2, 2026

The short version: Questling collects no data about you. Everything you put into the app stays on your device (and, if you turn on iCloud sync, in your own private iCloud). We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no way to see anything you do.

Who we are

Questling is an independent iOS app. For anything privacy-related, contact: coder420@blazedlabz.com.

What we collect

Nothing. Questling has no server and no user accounts. Your quests, notes, streaks, garden, companion conversations, and settings are stored only in the app's local database on your device.

iCloud sync (optional, off by default)

If you turn on iCloud Sync in Settings, your app data is stored in your personal, private iCloud database (Apple CloudKit) so it can sync between your own devices. This data is tied to your Apple Account, encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple, and is technically inaccessible to us. Turning sync off stops further syncing; you can delete iCloud app data at any time in iOS Settings → your name → iCloud.

Apple Health (optional, read-only)

If you enable HealthKit integration, Questling reads steps, workout minutes, mindful minutes, and sleep to adjust your daily energy suggestions and celebrate movement. This data is read on your device, is never written anywhere else, never leaves your device, and is never used for advertising or shared with anyone. You can revoke access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health.

Microphone & speech (optional)

The voice brain-dump feature uses your microphone and Apple's on-device speech recognition to turn your spoken thoughts into text. Audio and transcripts are processed entirely on your device and are not transmitted anywhere.

Location (optional)

If you allow location access, it is used once to show crisis helplines local to your region in the Safety Plan. Your location is not stored and never leaves your device.

The AI companion

Your companion is an AI, not a human. It runs on Apple's on-device foundation models (or a built-in scripted fallback on devices without them). Conversations are processed and stored only on your device (and in your private iCloud if sync is on). No conversation content is ever sent to us or any third party.

Notifications

Reminders and companion check-ins are scheduled locally on your device. We do not send remote push messages.

Children

Questling is not directed at children under 13. The companion is clearly disclosed as AI, and the Safety Plan provides crisis resources.

Your rights (GDPR & similar laws)

Because we collect and hold no personal data, there is nothing for us to access, correct, export, or erase on your behalf — you hold all of your data yourself. Deleting the app deletes its local data; iCloud data can be removed via iOS Settings as described above.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the new version will be posted at this address with an updated effective date. Any change will keep the same principle: your data stays yours.

Not medical care

Questling is a wellness tool. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you're struggling, please reach out to a clinician — and the app's Safety Plan can connect you with crisis resources.
